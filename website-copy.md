## FAQ

### Personas & Cast Members

**Q: What's a cast member?**
A: A cast member (aka persona) is an AI character with a specific perspective and expertise. The Skeptic questions everything. The Power User knows all the edge cases. The CTO thinks about scale and trade-offs. Pick the cast that fits your needs.

**Q: Can I create custom personas?**
A: Yes. Define your own cast members with specific backgrounds, expertise, and communication styles. Save them for reuse or share with your team.

**Q: How many cast members should I use?**
A: Depends on what you're reviewing. Quick sanity check? One cast member. Complex decision? Run 3-5 for diverse perspectives. You control the mix.

---

### Costs & Billing

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Free tier gets you a few tries on the website. After that, it's pass-through cost (server + AI tokens) plus a $0.05 fee only when running multiple cast members at once. Single cast member runs have no fee.

**Q: What's pass-through cost?**
A: We charge exactly what the AI tokens and server time cost. No markup. You'll see the breakdown before you run.

**Q: Do credits expire?**
A: Nope. Buy them, use them when you need them. No rush, no expiry.

**Q: Can I set spending limits?**
A: Yes. Set hard caps in your account settings. We'll stop processing when you hit them.

---

### Integrations

**Q: What integrations do you support?**
A: GitHub Actions is live. REST API for custom integrations. More coming—Slack, CLI, webhooks. Vote on what you want.

**Q: How does the GitHub Action work?**
A: Add the action to your workflow. It automatically runs your selected cast members on PRs, issues, or any artifact. Feedback posts as comments.

**Q: Can I use this in my own app?**
A: Yes. Full REST API with docs. Authentication via API keys. Rate limits apply based on your credit balance.

---

### Privacy & Data

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. Your uploads and feedback are private by default. We don't train on your data. Don't share it. Don't sell it.

**Q: How long do you keep my files?**
A: Configurable. Default is 30 days. You can set shorter retention or delete manually anytime.

**Q: What about sensitive content?**
A: Don't upload anything you wouldn't trust an AI with. For enterprise teams, dedicated instances and enhanced data controls are available.

---

### Export & Output

**Q: What formats can I export to?**
A: Markdown, JSON, PDF. Copy/paste works too. API returns structured JSON for programmatic access.

**Q: Can I save feedback for later?**
A: Yes. Your account has a library of all your runs. Search, filter, export, share.

**Q: How do I get notified?**
A: Email by default. Webhooks for integrations. GitHub comments if you're using the action.
